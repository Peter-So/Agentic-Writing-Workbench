# Quality Gates

Use these before delivery or when revising a chapter.

## Must pass

- Hook lands early.
- The chapter has a real event, not just atmosphere.
- Character actions fit their knowledge and position.
- Dialogue sounds distinct by role.
- The scene has function, not decoration.
- New information is earned, not dumped.
- End state changes something: goal, risk, relation, or knowledge.

## Continuity checks

- No repeated event from the previous chapter.
- No name or identity jump without evidence.
- No resource, skill, or relationship gains without a visible bridge.
- No world-rule violation unless the story explicitly shows the exception.

## Style checks

- Keep the chosen tone stable.
- Remove copied phrasing from source material.
- Cut filler that does not change event, relation, cost, or information.

## Word-count check

- Use the word-count script for markdown chapters.
- Treat target length as configurable.
- If a chapter is short, add linked action or consequence; do not pad with empty description.
